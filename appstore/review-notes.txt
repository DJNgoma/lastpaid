Last Paid does not require an account or login.

The core flow is:
1. Scan a product barcode with the camera, or enter the barcode manually.
2. Save the price paid, store, purchase date, and optional notes.
3. Open the saved product later to see the latest price, previous price, and history.

Camera access is only used for barcode scanning.
Location access is optional and is only used if the tester chooses to tag where a purchase was saved.
