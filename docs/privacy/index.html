Last Paid Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 22, 2026

Overview

Last Paid is a local-first shopping price tracker. The app is designed to help you remember what you last paid for a product by saving barcodes, product names, notes, and price history on your device.

Data Handling

Last Paid does not require an account and does not send your saved product history to a developer-run server. Information you enter in the app stays on your device unless you choose to share it using platform features outside the app in a future release.

Permissions

  • Camera access is used only to scan product barcodes.
  • Location access is optional and only used when you choose to tag where a price was saved.

Third-Party Tracking

Last Paid does not use third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

Contact

For the latest app support information, visit the Last Paid support page.
