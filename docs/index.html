Last Paid

Remember what you last paid.

Last Paid helps you scan a product barcode, save the price you paid, and check your latest price history the next time you shop.

Support

Last Paid is designed to be simple and local-first. If you run into trouble while testing, capture the screen where the issue happens and include the steps that led to it when you report it.

  • Share the product barcode or manual entry used.
  • Include the store, price, and date you entered.
  • Note whether the issue happened after scanning or manual entry.

Email support at info@africantechno.com for help, questions, or bug reports.

What the App Does

The app stores product names, barcodes, notes, and purchase prices so you can compare your recent prices over time.

Camera access is only used for barcode scanning. Location tagging is optional and only used when you choose to tag a purchase.
